About Excursion

Excursion is a travel application built for people who love discovering new places. The goal of the app is to help users explore nature, cities, and hidden locations that are often missed by traditional travel guides.

By using Excursion, travelers can keep track of places they enjoyed, plan future trips, and get inspired to explore more. The app focuses on simplicity, clarity, and adventure.


Excursion was designed with outdoor enthusiasts in mind, but it is also perfect for casual travelers. Whether you are camping, hiking, or just walking through a new city, Excursion helps you remember and rediscover amazing places.

Our mission is to encourage exploration and curiosity while keeping the experience simple and enjoyable for everyone.
